About Me

I'm Ryan Lamar, a songwriter and multi-instrumentalist musician. After over thirty years dedicated solely to the guitar I began a journey of exploration musically that brought me new worlds of music. Nearly in my forties, I began studying banjo, mandolin, violin and piano. Reading music, studying theory and song structure and a lifelong passion for the craft of songwriting, I became fascinated with the challenge of acquiring new skills later in life. Beyond structure is musicality, beneath the forms lay the inspirations that create them.
